OLED vs. QLED: Choosing Your Perfect TCL TV

Demystifying the display technologies to help you make an informed decision for your home. When selecting a TCL TV, understanding the difference between OLED and QLED is essential. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) offers perfect blacks and infinite contrast by individually lighting each pixel, making it ideal for cinema lovers and dark-room viewing. On the other hand, QLED (Quantum Dot LED) TVs use a backlight and a quantum dot layer to deliver brighter images and more vivid colors ‚Äî a great choice for well-lit rooms and sports enthusiasts. While OLED delivers exceptional depth and realism, QLED excels in durability and brightness. Your perfect TCL TV depends on your viewing habits, room lighting, and what matters most to you: cinematic immersion or vibrant clarity.

The Ideal Screen Size for Your Kenyan Living Room

Tips and tricks to pick the best TV size for your space and viewing distance. Choosing the right screen size depends on the distance between your seating area and where your TV will be placed. In most Kenyan living rooms, where space may be modest, a 43" to 55" TCL TV is often ideal for about 2.5 to 3 meters viewing distance. For larger rooms or open layouts, you can comfortably go for a 65" or even 75" screen. A good rule of thumb: divide your viewing distance (in inches) by 1.6 to get an ideal screen size. Bigger isn‚Äôt always better‚Äîclarity, comfort, and space harmony matter too!
